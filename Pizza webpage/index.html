About Us

Our Story

Chef’s Pizza is not your average pizzeria. Founded in 2002, it combines quality, values and work ethic with ambiance and award winning appeal. They always cared about the traditions, passion for excellence and commitment to quality that makes Chef’s Pizza what it is today.

After working in some of Italy’s finest pizzerias for years, our chefs opened their first location New York. Refusing to use frozen dough, conveyor ovens, canned vegetables or many other shortcuts, our chefs set the standards for their award-winning pizza.

The Pizza

Today, our chefs continue to make fresh dough daily from scratch. Produce is delivered 6 days a week to ensure only the freshest ingredients possible. They use nothing but fresh toppings, as they know there is no substitute for quality. While quality, handmade pizza created in the classic New York tradition is the cornerstone of Chef's, the ambiance of the restaurant adds to its appeal.

Casual and stylish, the restaurant's a warm chic take on a contemporary vibe. Designed with cool fixtures and unexpectedly elegant twists, this is the place you can go for a glass of wine with friends or family.

We encourage you to come watch our skilled artisans as they hand stretch and toss dough, show their magic while your pizza's getting prepared in a traditional brick oven.
